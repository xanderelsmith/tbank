# I Just made 10000k dollars to myself as a Web3 Developer

*(Hook)*
I just minted $10,000 instantly into my own Web3 wallet, and I'm going to show you exactly how you can build a full crypto banking app that does the exact same thing—without writing complex backend smart contracts.

*(Body)*
Most developers think Web3 is too hard. They spend months learning Solidity, struggling with gas fees, and trying to figure out how to bridge fiat to crypto. But today, we're skipping all of that. 

Using Flutter and the **Toronet SDK**, we're taking a completely static, beautiful UI template and turning it into a functional decentralized bank in under 10 minutes.

Here is what we're going to build:
1. **Wallet Generation:** Creating a secure Web3 wallet with a 12-word seed phrase locally on the device.
2. **Multi-Asset Balances:** Fetching balances for fiat-pegged stablecoins like USD, NGN, EUR, and native gas tokens with a single line of code.
3. **The "Cheat Code":** Using Toronet's testnet API to instantly mint and fund our app with thousands of testnet dollars so we can test the system without spending real money.
4. **Real-World Utility:** Wiring up bank withdrawals, virtual mastercards, and cross-chain bridging—features that usually require massive development teams.
